# Peace lily (Spathiphyllum Starlight)

**Family**: Araceae
**Category**: Spathiphyllum
**Origin**: Cultivar

### Light Requirements
Bright light (Tolerated: Diffused)

### Watering
Keep moist between watering. Must not be dry between watering

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Mealy bug, Thrips
* **Diseases**: Phytophtora

### Uses
Table top, Tertiary

### Growth & Fertilizer
* **Growth**: moderate
* **Soil**: well-drained
* **Fertilizer**: None
