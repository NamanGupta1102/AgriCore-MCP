# Elephant ear (Alocasias X Amazonica)

**Family**: Araceae
**Category**: Other
**Origin**: Hybrid

### Light Requirements
Bright light (Tolerated: Diffused)

### Watering
Keep moist between watering. Water when soil is half dry.

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Spider mite, Mealy bug
* **Diseases**: ['Phytophtora', 'Crown rot']

### Uses
Colors / Forms, Potted plant, Primary
