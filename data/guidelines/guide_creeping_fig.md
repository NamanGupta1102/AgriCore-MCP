# Creeping fig (Ficus pumila)

**Family**: Moraceae
**Category**: Ficus
**Origin**: Cultivar

### Light Requirements
6 or more hours of direct sunlight per day. (Tolerated: Direct sunlight.)

### Watering
Keep moist between watering. Must not be dry between watering

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Spider mite, Mealy bug, Scale
* **Diseases**: N/A

### Uses
