# Variegated Euphorbia (Euphorbia ammack)

**Family**: Euphorbiaceae
**Category**: Cactus And Succulent
**Origin**: Africa

### Light Requirements
6 or more hours of direct sunlight per day. (Tolerated: Direct sunlight.)

### Watering
Must be dry between watering. Water only when dry.

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Mealy bug, White fly
* **Diseases**: Downy mildiou

### Uses
Potted plant, Primary
