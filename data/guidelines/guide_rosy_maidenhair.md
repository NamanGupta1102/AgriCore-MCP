# Rosy Maidenhair (Adiantum hispidulum)

**Family**: Polypodiaceae
**Category**: Fern
**Origin**: Australia & New Guinea

### Light Requirements
Bright light (Tolerated: Diffused)

### Watering
Keep moist between watering. Must not be dry between watering

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Mealy bug, Aphid, Snail
* **Diseases**: Gray mold

### Uses
Table top, Ground cover, Tertiary
