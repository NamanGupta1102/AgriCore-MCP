# Golden Pothos (Epipremnum aureum)

**Family**: Araceae
**Category**: Hanging
**Origin**: South Pacific

### Light Requirements
Bright light (Tolerated: Diffused)

### Watering
Keep moist between watering. Water when soil is half dry.

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Mealy bug
* **Diseases**: ['Pythium']

### Uses
Hanging, Tertiary
