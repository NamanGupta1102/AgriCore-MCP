# Fernleaf Philodendron (Philodendron pinnatifidum)

**Family**: Araceae
**Category**: Philodendron
**Origin**: Venezuela

### Light Requirements
Bright light (Tolerated: Diffused)

### Watering
Keep moist between watering. Water when soil is half dry.

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Mealy bug
* **Diseases**: Erwinia

### Uses
Table top, Tertiary
