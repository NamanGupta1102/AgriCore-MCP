# Tailflower (Anthurium X)

**Family**: Araceae
**Category**: Anthurium
**Origin**: Cultivar

### Light Requirements
Bright light (Tolerated: Diffused)

### Watering
Water when soil is half dry. Can be dry between watering.

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Spider mite, Mealy bug, Aphid
* **Diseases**: ['Erwinia', 'Crown rot']

### Uses
Flower, Table top, Tertiary
