# Chinese Evergreen (Aglaonema)

**Family**: Araceae
**Category**: Aglaonema
**Origin**: Hybrid

### Light Requirements
Bright light (Tolerated: Diffused)

### Watering
Keep moist between watering. Water when soil is half dry.

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Mealy bug, Scale
* **Diseases**: ['Phytophtora', 'Crown rot']

### Uses
Potted plant, Secondary, Tertiary

### Growth & Fertilizer
* **Growth**: slow
* **Soil**: well-drained
* **Fertilizer**: None
