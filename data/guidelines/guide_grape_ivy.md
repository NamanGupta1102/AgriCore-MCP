# Grape Ivy (Cissus rhombifolia)

**Family**: Vitaceae
**Category**: Hanging
**Origin**: Central America & South America

### Light Requirements
Bright light (Tolerated: Diffused)

### Watering
Keep moist between watering. Can dry between watering

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Mealy bug
* **Diseases**: ['Downy mildiou', 'Powdery']

### Uses
Hanging, Ground cover
