# Velvet leaf vine (Philodendron micans)

**Family**: Araceae
**Category**: Philodendron
**Origin**: Dominica

### Light Requirements
Bright light (Tolerated: Diffused)

### Watering
Keep moist between watering. Water when soil is half dry.

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Mealy bug
* **Diseases**: Erwinia

### Uses
Hanging, Secondary
