# Dumcane (Dieffenbachia amoena)

**Family**: Araceae
**Category**: Dieffenbachia
**Origin**: Cultivar

### Light Requirements
Bright light (Tolerated: Diffused)

### Watering
Keep moist between watering. Can dry between watering

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Spider mite, Mealy bug
* **Diseases**: ['Fusarium', 'Erwinia']

### Uses
Potted plant, Secondary
