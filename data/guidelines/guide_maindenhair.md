# Maindenhair (Adiantum raddianum)

**Family**: Polypodiaceae
**Category**: Fern
**Origin**: Brazil

### Light Requirements
Bright light (Tolerated: Diffused)

### Watering
Keep moist between watering. Must not be dry between watering

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Mealy bug, Aphid, Snail
* **Diseases**: Gray mold

### Uses
Potted plant, Ground cover, Table top
