# Giant Candelabra tree (Euphorbia ingens)

**Family**: Euphorbiaceae
**Category**: Cactus And Succulent
**Origin**: South Africa

### Light Requirements
6 or more hours of direct sunlight per day. (Tolerated: Direct sunlight.)

### Watering
Water only when the soil is dry. Must be dry between watering

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Mealy bug
* **Diseases**: Downy mildiou

### Uses
Potted plant, Primary
