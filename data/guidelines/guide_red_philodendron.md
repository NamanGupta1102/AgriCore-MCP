# Red Philodendron (Philodendron)

**Family**: Araceae
**Category**: Philodendron
**Origin**: Cultivar

### Light Requirements
Bright light (Tolerated: Diffused)

### Watering
Keep moist between watering. Water when soil is half dry.

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Mealy bug
* **Diseases**: Erwinia
