# Tree Cereus (Cereus peruvianus)

**Family**: Cactaceae
**Category**: Cactus And Succulent
**Origin**: Brazil

### Light Requirements
6 or more hours of direct sunlight per day. (Tolerated: Direct sunlight.)

### Watering
Water only when dry or when soil is half dry.

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Scale, Mealy bug
* **Diseases**: N/A

### Uses
Potted plant, Primary
