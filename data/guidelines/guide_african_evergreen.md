# African evergreen (Syngonium podophyllum)

**Family**: Araceae
**Category**: Hanging
**Origin**: Cultivar

### Light Requirements
Bright light (Tolerated: Diffused)

### Watering
Keep moist between watering. Water when soil is half dry.

### Common Issues
* **Insects**: Spider mite, Mealy bug
* **Diseases**: ['Crown rot', 'Erwinia']

### Uses
Hanging, Tertiary
